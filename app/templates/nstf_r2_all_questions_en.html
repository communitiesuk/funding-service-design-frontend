3.6.1.1. Night shelter or emergency accommodation

Is the night shelter or emergency accommodation communal?

Is the night shelter or emergency accommodation single room accommodation?

A single room is separate living accommodation. It has a lockable door, four walls and a ceiling, secure window and appropriate ventilation.  It also has access to cooking facilities and full bathroom facilities (single-use or shared). Cooking facilities are required unless this is explicitly indicated as unnecessary, for example in fully catered provision.
