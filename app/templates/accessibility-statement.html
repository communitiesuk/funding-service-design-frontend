Accessibility statement

The Department of Levelling Up, Housing and Communities is committed to making our online services accessible to all users by meeting The Public Sector Bodies (Websites and Mobile Applications) (No. 2) Accessibility Regulations 2018. We have commissioned The Digital Accessibility Centre (DAC) to carry out an accessibility audit of our services. This will include:

  • Web content accessibility guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 AA level technical compliance audits
  • extensive manual testing

The services booked in for testing are:

  • Community ownership fund online application
  • Community ownership fund online assessment

Accessibility testing will be complete by December 2022.
