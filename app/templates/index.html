Community Ownership Fund

Apply for funding to save a building in your community

Before you start

You must have received an invitation to apply. If we did not invite you, first express your interest in the fund.

Read the fund's prospectus before you apply.

When you must apply

You must submit this form and all supporting documents by midday on {date} 2022.

Who can apply

Applying with other groups

You can apply jointly with other community groups.

One of the groups must take responsibility for completing and submitting the application.

If we award you funding, only the group that submitted will sign the grant agreement with us.

Number of applications for each group

You can apply just once for each community asset or amenity.

How to complete your application

Moving through the form

This form is split into sections based on how it will be assessed. You do not have to complete it in order, or all at once.

When you have written something in every field in a section, the section will be marked as complete. You can still change your responses in sections that are marked as complete.

Making changes

If you need to edit or revisit any sections while you’re filling in the application, use the browser back button. The screen you’re editing will not save automatically, so select Save and continue before you go back.

You can edit your responses as many times as you like before you submit.

Submitting your application

When you have completed all sections, you’ll be able to submit your application.

Once you have submitted your application, you’ll not be able to edit, add or change any sections.
